Brand Identity Design - Hunter High Performance

Project Description

Looking for a logo design for a new start up business - Hunter High Performance. Hunter High Performance offers ECU Remapping / Engine tuning to unleash your daily driver's potential. Increase the output of your engine without voiding the warranty! Some key messages: DIESEL EXPERTS TO YOUR DOOR ECU REMAPPING & TUNING AND CUSTOM MODIFICATIONS GET TUNED! We have a draft concept that needs building on and stylizing. Also open to brand new designs. We don't want to logo to be too complex as it will need to translate to car stickers, t-shirts, stubby coolers and other promotion material. Easily read from a distance or speed.
